# Recruitment Screener

## Recruit Target

Recruit 18-24 participants total for three 90-minute workshops.

Session mix:

1. Solo frequent users: 6-8 participants.
2. Household orderers: 6-8 participants.
3. Group orderers: 6-8 participants.

## Screening Questions

### Eligibility

1. Are you 18 years or older?
   - Must answer yes.

2. Which city or area do you live in?
   - Prioritize current or likely Gawula launch areas.

3. Which food delivery apps have you used in the last 30 days?
   - Uber Eats.
   - Mr D.
   - Both.
   - Other.
   - Must include Uber Eats or Mr D.

4. How many times have you ordered food delivery in the last 30 days?
   - 0-1: reject.
   - 2: backup.
   - 3-5: accept.
   - 6+: accept.

5. How often do you order for more than one person?
   - Never.
   - Sometimes.
   - Often.
   - Almost always.

6. In the last 60 days, have you had any of these moments?
   - Ordered for people who wanted different restaurants.
   - Wanted drinks, dessert, snacks, or groceries after choosing food.
   - Placed more than one delivery order for the same meal occasion.
   - Paid more than one delivery fee for related items.
   - Had a late, missing, wrong, cold, or cancelled order.
   - Had to contact support about a food delivery order.

Accept participants who match at least one moment. Prioritize people who match two or more.

### Segmentation

7. Which best describes your common ordering situation?
   - Mostly ordering for myself.
   - Ordering for my household.
   - Ordering for coworkers/classmates/friends.
   - Mixed.

8. What matters most when choosing a delivery app?
   - Cheapest total.
   - Fastest delivery.
   - Best restaurant choice.
   - Promos/deals.
   - Reliable support/refunds.
   - Familiar restaurants.
   - Payment options.

9. Which statement sounds most like you?
   - I usually know exactly what I want.
   - I browse until something looks good.
   - I choose based on deals.
   - I choose based on what the group will accept.
   - I choose based on delivery time.

10. Are you comfortable joining a 90-minute recorded group discussion about food delivery apps?
    - Must answer yes.

## Exclusions

Exclude participants who:

1. Work for Uber Eats, Mr D, Takealot, delivery competitors, restaurant delivery operations, or market research agencies.
2. Have not used Uber Eats or Mr D recently.
3. Cannot discuss specific recent ordering experiences.
4. Are unable to participate for the full session.

## Incentive

Offer a meaningful food-delivery voucher or cash equivalent. Keep the incentive neutral and not tied to Gawula usage.

## Confirmation Message

Hi [Name], thanks for agreeing to join our food delivery research session. We are speaking to Uber Eats and Mr D customers about how food delivery apps could work better. The session is 90 minutes, will be recorded for research notes, and your feedback will be anonymized. We are not testing your knowledge and there are no wrong answers. Please bring one recent food delivery experience to discuss.
