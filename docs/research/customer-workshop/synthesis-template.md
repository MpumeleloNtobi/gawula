## Trust Requirements

Before launching any same-hub multi-store flow, customers must understand:

1. Which stores are in the basket.
2. Whether the order arrives together.
3. What happens if one store is delayed.
4. Whether there is one delivery fee or multiple fees.
5. Who fixes missing or wrong items.
6. How refund decisions work.
7. What proof confirms delivery.
